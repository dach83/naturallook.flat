Want to learn more about how we work?
Ah good, then you’re in the right place.

We like to get to know you first

Before getting carried away with sketches we like to find out the ins and outs of your business. Once we know what you do, how you do it and who you do it for we can make the most of your marketing material.

Working on your branding

Your logo design is the shop window to your business so it’s important to get it right. We create unique and clever brands for businesses of all shapes and sizes. Read more about our process below or get in touch to discuss your new logo design now.

1

We’ll ask you some important questions, they'll help us to understand your business, your market and your aspirations.

2

Now that we know how your business needs to present itself we’ll produce a range of logo designs that best suit your market.

3

Once developed and you’re 100% happy we can show you how best to use your fantastic new logo to your advantage.

Designing for print

We work with trusted printers to produce great quality products including anything from flyers and brochures to banners and signage. See more about how we work below or get in touch to discuss your next project.

1

We’ll ask you some questions. We know that what you ‘want’ isn’t always what you ‘need’ or what suits your budget, so we’ll work that out with you.

2

Working with images and copy supplied by you (or by our specialists) we’ll work on creating the very best design solution for you and your market.

3

Once we're finished we’ll send the design off to our good friends at the printers. They’ll deliver the finished product direct to you or your customers.

Creating your website

We’re proud of our responsive website designs, they’re clever and easy to use (at both ends). We ensure that your website is mobile optimised, totally unique, and tailored to suit your business. Learn more about how we design websites below or get in touch to discuss your new website.

1

We’ll ask you some general questions; like “what pages do you need” but we'll also investigate what you want to achieve with your new website.

2

Using this information together with our own market research we’ll develop some designs that best suit your goals for the website.

3

We'll sort out your hosting and walk you through your new CMS. We’ll even give your site a 6 month review to see how you’re getting on.
